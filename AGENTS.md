# Landing Page

A landing page written in Next.js, deployed to Vercel and styled consistently like `Alvin Chiew Portfolio` in local repository `../portfolio-alvinchiew/` or [website](https://alvinchiew.com).

This page includes sections below:

- Project A3 introduction
- Tools showcase with description, download link, video link
- Sign up for activation code. Obtained by filling a minimal sign up form which consists of Name, Business email, Company, Industry
- Entire Contact section from Alvin Chiew Portfolio
  - Email section for business inquiry which consists of Your email, Subject, Message
  - Contacts & channel links
